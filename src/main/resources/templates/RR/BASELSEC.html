Clarifying Notes
(1) For definitions of all terms, and instructions on calculations, banks should refer to the Central Bank's draft Standard on Required Capital for Securitizations.
(2) Note that the template has separate, mutually exclusive blocks for securitization exposures that meet the "Simple, Transparent, and Comparable" (STC) criteria, resecuritization exposures, traditional exposures, and synthetic exposures (with the last two block excluding any exposures that are reported in the STC or resecuritization blocks).
(3) Report in the STC block only those exposures that fully meet the criteria established in the Annexes to the Securitization Standard.
(4) Note that Resecuritizations (exposures to securitizations for which the pool includes some securitized exposures) may not be treated under the external ratings based approach.
(5) For the Traditional Securitization block, report only the amounts for exposures that have NOT been reported as STC.
(6) For the Synthetic Securitization block, report only the amounts for exposures that have NOT been reported as resecuritizations.
(7) Note that Investor and Non-Investor exposures should be reported separately within each block.
