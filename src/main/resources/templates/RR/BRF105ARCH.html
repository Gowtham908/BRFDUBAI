Explanatory Notes
a. Consolidation level follows the applicable capital adequacy standards for banks in the UAE and the corresponding banking return form (BRF 95).
b. Item no. 1, 2, 3 and 4 are inclusive of all data points mentioned under item no.
c. Cells G61, I61, K61, M61, E63, O63, Q63 are to be reported on a best effort basis.
1) Exposures are to be reported on a gross basis (including interest in suspense) as per the applicable capital adequacy standards for banks in the UAE and the corresponding banking return form (BRF 95).
2) CRWA of Exposures are as per the applicable capital adequacy standards for banks in the UAE.
3) Non-performing exposures as per Circular No 28/2010 Regulation for the Classification of Loans and their Provisions.
4) Defaulted Exposures are exposures that are more than 90 days past due (or, if applicable, the bank considers that the obligor is unlikely to pay its credit obligations in full) as per the applicable capital adequacy standards for banks in the UAE.
5) Real Estate Loans/Financing to Individuals for plots shall be covered under item 1.2.
6) Real estate Loans/Financing to Corporates includes real estate developers, GREs, HNIs, SMEs, and all other non-retail exposures. The definition of SMEs follows the corresponding bank return form (BRF 74).
7) Item no. 5 represent off-balance sheet (unfunded) gross exposures and CRWA of exposures reported as per the applicable capital adequacy standards for banks in the UAE.
8) Total CRWA of total bank risk-weighted assets per the applicable capital adequacy standards for banks in the UAE and the corresponding banking return form (BRF 95).
9) Total Gross Outstanding Assets shall be reported on a gross basis, including interest in suspense.
10) Total Gross Off-balance Sheet Exposure shall be reported on a gross basis, including interest in suspense.
